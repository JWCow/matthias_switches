Evolution of Mechanical Switches

Explore the history and development of mechanical keyboard switches through the years.

1984

Cherry MX Switches

Cherry introduces the MX switch series, setting the standard for mechanical keyboard switches. The Cherry MX Blue becomes iconic for its clicky feedback.

2010s

Rise of Gateron

Gateron emerges as a major competitor, offering smoother switches at competitive prices. The Gateron Yellow becomes a community favorite.

2017

Premium Switches Era

Zealios and Holy Panda switches introduce premium tactile experiences, revolutionizing the enthusiast market with their unique feel.

2020+

Modern Innovation

New manufacturers push boundaries with innovative designs, offering features like factory lubrication and unique stem designs.
